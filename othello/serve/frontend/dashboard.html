Othello AlphaZero — Training Dashboard

Each iteration the agent plays a batch of games against itself, trains its neural network on them, then is tested on a ladder of minimax opponents. These charts track that loop over time. Every chart says, in plain terms, what it measures and what "getting better" looks like.
